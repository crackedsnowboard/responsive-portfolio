About Me

Beginning coder with previous experience as an accountant and cybersecurity. My primary goal is to develop modern web applications to help solve relevant problems. A secondary goal is to understand how web applications are built in order to help secure them better. Underlying all of this is just a fascination with coding and the enjoyment of constantly learning new frameworks/technologies. I currently live in the San Francisco Bay area and have previously lived in Washington DC and Jakarta Indonesia. I enjoy cycling and skiing in the winter, but I’m not sure if any of this is still relevant post Covid - but I remain hopeful. I enjoy traveling and recently went to India where I finally learned to make the family beef and fish curry recipes. I also enjoy learning languages and speak Bahasa Indonesian and Mandarin Chinese. My greatest accomplishment was riding my bike from Seattle to Vancouver which was over 200 miles and we did it in just two days. Some how I gained weight over that weekend.
